# Arbitration Application Drafter

## Overview

Draft a labor arbitration application from structured facts and evidence. The output is a draft for review, not legal representation or a guaranteed filing result.

## Required Information

- 申请人身份信息；
- 被申请人准确主体信息；
- 劳动关系基本事实；
- 仲裁请求和金额；
- 事实与理由；
- 证据目录。

## Output Requirements

1. Clearly mark the document as "draft".
2. Use placeholders for missing identity, employer entity, address, claim amount, dates, and evidence.
3. Separate claims, facts and reasons, evidence list, and review notes.
4. Add human-review reminders for amount, jurisdiction, limitation period, employer entity, claim strategy, and legal basis.
5. Do not fabricate statutes, evidence, documents, facts, signatures, or expected outcomes.

## Guardrails

- Use cautious wording for contested or jurisdiction-specific issues.
- Cite official sources where possible, and point local filing requirements to the relevant city/province arbitration commission or public legal service channel with update date.
- If facts are insufficient, produce a preparation checklist instead of a polished filing draft.
